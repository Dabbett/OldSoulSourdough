About Us

My name is Tiana, the proud owner and artisan baker behind this cozy cottage bakery.

Old Soul Sourdough is born out of a passion for crafting exceptional sourdough products using a starter that I've nurtured and cultivated organically, starting from just flour and water. Every loaf that emerges from our oven tells a story of patience, dedication and attention to detail.

In this little cottage bakery, I take pride in creating more than just bread; I am crafting experiences that evoke the warmth and nostalgia of home. Each batch is lovingly prepared with a commitment to quality and tradition.

Our sourdough journey is a celebration of simplicity, authenticity, and the timeless connection between ingredients and the hands that shape them. From classic sourdough loaves to delightful variations, we offer a range of baked goods that capture the essence of old-world baking with a contemporary twist. Thank you for being a part of the Old Soul Sourdough family.
